My name is Chrisitana Noray,
I’m a passionate animal lover and environmentalists
who believes in Planetary Rights.
I consider myself a ‘forever student,’
Driven on building my academic foundations
in both Veterinary Medicine and Environmental Conservation.
My hunger for knowledge and determination to turn information
into action has contributed to pursuing a Doctrine Veterinary Medical degree
as well as becoming involved in environmental protection acts.
As a young ambitious first generation Trinidadian emigrant,
I’m primed to take on the domain. In my spare times I indulge in leisurely activities .
Those hobbies include writing and reading poetry, listening to a wide range of music
from classical to modern, as well as playing tennis.
These humble luxuries are just the start to a long list
of others that will be engraved into my selfhood ultimately.
